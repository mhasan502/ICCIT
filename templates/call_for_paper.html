Call for Paper

ICCIT 2021 will provide a forum for researchers and professionals from both academia and industry to exchange up-to-date knowledge and experience in the field of Computer and Electrical Engineering. Prospective authors are invited to submit original technical papers for presentation at the conference. ICCIY 2021 will be held from December 18-20, 2021 in a virtual mode. All accepted and presented papers will be submitted for inclusion in IEEE XploreÂ® Digital Library (pending approval).
