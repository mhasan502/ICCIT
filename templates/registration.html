Submission Method:

Each of the accepted papers should have a MP4 presentation video (maximum 10 minutes and maximum 250 MB size). Please save it as Paper_ID.mp4 (for example for paper 12, save it as 12.mp4). A presenter must register and upload MP4 video link (for example, Google Drive or Drop Box link) and provide it in Google Form. This video will be played during the technical session but the presenter must take part in the interactive Q&A session.


The deadline of payment and presentar registration with mp4 video link is 19 Nov, 11:59 PM Bangladesh Standard Time (BST), 2021

The payment confirmation will be emailed to the corresponding author from the EasyChair Submission System after 28th November, 2021.
